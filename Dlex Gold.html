Dlex Gold

Julius Daniel Ayomide (born 12 September 2006), popularly known as Dlex Gold, is a Nigerian gospel singer and songwriter. Dlex released his first single in October 2023 titled "Over N Over" and rose to prominence with the 2024 song "T.G.F.G.F".
